Alesia Greenleaf (Dryad of the Tree)

Hp 40/40 AC: 17 Movement: 30ft Resistances:

Vulnerabilities: Fire

Weapons: Leafblade: Range 5ft, +7 to hit, 10 slashing damage.

    Barb Bolt: Range 30/60ft, +8 to hit, 10 Piercing damage.

Str: 10 (+0), Dex: 14(+2), Con:14 (+2), Wis:16 (+3), Int:12 (+1), Cha:14 (+2)         Save Proficiencies: Wis: +7, Dex: +6

Proficiencies: Nature +12, Medicine +12, Cooking +12, History +6, Arcana +6

  

Spellcasting:

At will: Druidcraft

3/day: Goodberry, Cure wounds (2nd Level), Earthbind, Wither Bloom

1/day: Create food and drink, Plant Growth, Entangle

  

Ideals:   I am part Fae, part human I live between two worlds and will explore both.

I love nature, I tend the garden and ensure it flourishes.

Bonds:  I my tree is my life; this ship is my freedom.

Flaws:   I am immortal… time isn’t something I am used to.
