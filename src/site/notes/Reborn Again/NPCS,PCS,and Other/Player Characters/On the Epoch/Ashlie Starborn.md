Primary Gunner: Ashlie Starborn (Kor Farseer)
Ashlie Starborn (Kor Farseer)

Hp 35/35 AC: 18 Movement: 30ft Resistances: Radiant and Nectrotic

Vulnerabilities:

Weapons: Kor Hooks: Range 5ft, two targets, +7 to hit, 8 radiant  damage.

    Hieromatic Rifle: Range 240/480ft, +8 to hit, 15 radiant  damage. Reload 1.

  

Proficiencies: Arcana +12, Survival +12, Tool Proficiency (Kor-Tech) +12, History +6, Perception +6

  

Child of the Light: As a spirit dancer Ashlie has a unique understanding of the magic of nature. Once per day she can either restore a total of 45 hitpoints from allies within 15ft or deal 15 radiant damage to any creatures within 10ft.

Kor-tech trained: Ashlie can repair and maintain all kor-tech.

Latent Precognition: 3/day, Ashlie may force a creature she sees within 60ft to reroll a attack roll or saving throw.

  

Ideals:   I am here to kill demons and save someone, who I dont know

I enjoy my job, this gun is fun.

Bonds:  I am not sure why I am here but I know it is important.

Flaws:   Past, present and future have no meaning there is only now and other now.
